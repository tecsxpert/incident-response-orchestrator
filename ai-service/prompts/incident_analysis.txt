Analyze the following security incident and provide a comprehensive assessment:

1. **Threat Classification**: Identify the type of threat (malware, data breach, DDoS, insider threat, etc.)
2. **Severity Level**: Assess the severity (Critical, High, Medium, Low)
3. **Affected Systems**: List all potentially affected systems and assets
4. **Timeline**: Establish the incident timeline if available
5. **Root Cause Analysis**: Determine the likely root cause or attack vector
6. **Impact Assessment**: Evaluate the business and security impact
7. **Immediate Actions**: Recommend immediate containment steps

Be concise, factual, and actionable in your analysis.
